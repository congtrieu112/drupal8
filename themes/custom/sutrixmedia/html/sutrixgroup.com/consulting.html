About Sutrix Consulting

About Sutrix Consulting

Sutrix Consulting currently has 2 offices in Copenhagen and Paris. We provide technology strategy & consulting for multinationals who requires scalable, maintainable, global digital systems. Our consultant will work with your team to gather requirements and design optimized digital solutions for maximum impact & ROI.

As companies now require global apps optimized to work across devices and regions, our consultant will tailor design your application’s UX with the appropriate front-end and back-end frameworks for your business needs.

We can also provide on-site consulting, training and development as needed.
