About Sutrix Group

Sutrix Group offers on-site digital consultancy and digital production services globally. Our strength is the end-to-end capability to design & develop global technology platforms and provide ongoing support & maintenance services.

As of 2015, Sutrix Group has over 350 staff in 4 countries to service our worldwide clients.

Sutrix Group has two subsidiaries:

Sutrix Consulting: Provides on-site consultants for application specifications and designing complex digital technology platforms.

Sutrix Solutions: Provides web & mobile development with ongoing support & maintenance.
